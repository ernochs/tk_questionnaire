THOMAS-KILMANN COFLICT MODES: Interpreting Your Scores

Your profile of scores indicates the repertoire of conflict handing skills that you possess ad use in conflict situations. One of the most often asked questions is “What are the right answers?” in this type of test, there are no “right” answers. All five modes of handing conflict are useful in various situation, and each represent a set of useful social skills. The effectives of any handing of conflict depends on the conflict and the skill that is employed.

Each of us is capable of using all the five conflict modes, and none of us can be characterized as having a single rigid style of dealing with conflict. However, because of personality traits or by habit, individuals tend to use one or two modes at a greater frequency than the others.
